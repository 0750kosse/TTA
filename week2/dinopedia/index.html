Key Facts

  1. Dinosaurs were around millions of years ago!
  2. There were more than 700 species.
  3. The word dinosaur came from an English palaeontologist.
  4. Tyrannosaurus Rex was the most ferocious dinosaur.
  5. Lizards, turtles, snakes and crocodiles all descend from dinosaurs.
